Employer Route Methodology

Production Atlas separates general company research routes from confirmed event-specific employer or vendor connections.

HomeGuideOpportunitiesCalendarMapEmployersSources

General employer routes

The Employers page lists public company, staffing, vendor, venue, promoter, producer, and organization routes that may be relevant to live-event production work. These are useful research starting points, but they are not automatically tied to a specific festival.

Event-specific routes

A company should appear inside a festival popup only when public information connects that company or route to that specific event. When that connection is not available, Production Atlas keeps the general company route on the Employers page instead of implying an event relationship.

Apply pages

Apply or careers links point to official public pages where workers can begin the company’s normal hiring or roster process.

Contact pages

Contact links point to official public company or organization pages when a direct apply page is not available.

IATSE/local routing

Local-jurisdiction references must use cautious language unless a current public source supports a specific claim. Preferred wording: verify applicable IATSE/local jurisdiction before outreach.

Safety boundary

Do not upgrade a public company, employer, vendor, producer, venue, or local-jurisdiction lead into a confirmed event relationship unless the public source supports that specific connection.
