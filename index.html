Research workflow

Questions for every festival

  • Who builds the stage?
  • Who supplies rigging?
  • Who supplies lighting?
  • Who supplies audio?
  • Who supplies video/LED?
  • Who handles site ops?
  • Who handles power?
  • Which IATSE local applies?
  • Which nonunion labor companies staff the event?
  • Who is the production manager or production company?

Minimum useful record

  • Festival name, city, state, month/window, producer/promoter.
  • Production departments active at the event.
  • Known or likely employer channels by department.
  • Union/local jurisdiction notes.
  • Nonunion labor company leads.
  • Source URL and last verification date.
  • Status: confirmed, needs verification, lead only, historical, or user supplied.
