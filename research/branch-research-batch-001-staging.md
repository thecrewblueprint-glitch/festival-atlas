## Research Question

For each opportunity, answer:

> Who builds or controls the stage, roof/deck systems, temporary structures, and local staging labor route?

This batch does **not** mark any staging vendor as confirmed unless a public source directly ties that vendor to the specific event.

| Label | Meaning |
