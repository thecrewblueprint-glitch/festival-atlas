
until each target has been individually verified from public sources and intentionally promoted into the live dataset.

## Before any target goes live

Each target needs a research pass that confirms, from public sources:

1. Official festival identity.
2. Official 2026 or 2027 dates, or a clear pending-date status.
3. City, state, and venue/site when public.
4. Camping availability or camping/grounds relevance.
5. Public producer/promoter/operator when available.
6. Public ticket/contact/apply/volunteer/vendor/staff route when available.
7. Production departments likely involved.
8. Public-safe source URL for centralized `sources.html` use.
9. No private contacts, pay rates, lodging details, crew rumors, or non-public vendor lists.

If a field cannot be confirmed, use:

```text
Unknown publicly. Human verification needed.
```
