# Research Batch 002 — Major National Anchor Targets

Date: 2026-06-21  
Branch: research-version  
Targets: Coachella, Stagecoach, EDC Las Vegas, Bonnaroo, Austin City Limits

---

## Scoring Method

This batch scores opportunity value based on work-year usefulness:

- lodging / housing signal
- paid travel / mileage / flight signal
- per diem signal
- work-window length
- hiring route clarity
- repeat or multi-market value
- source confidence

The score does not measure event popularity. It measures whether the target is likely to help fill open time in the work year.

## Batch Findings

Recommended score: **62**

Reasoning:

- Official site includes hotel-package and shuttle-related public signals, but these are attendee-facing and not confirmed worker lodging or worker travel.

Recommended score: **54**

### 3. EDC Las Vegas

Reasoning:

### 4. Bonnaroo

Recommended score: **56**

Reasoning:

- Official site confirms Manchester, TN, June 11–14, 2026.
- Official site describes Bonnaroo as a music and friendship world held on its 700-acre farm.
- Official navigation includes accommodations and hotel booking, but this is attendee-facing.
- Rural camping-festival format likely creates large site ops, staging, power, logistics, and camping infrastructure demand, but hiring route is still not public enough.

Next human action:

Verify Bonnaroo vendor stack, local/staging labor path, and whether camping or lodging access exists for paid crew separate from attendee accommodations.

### 5. Austin City Limits

Recommended score: **58**

Reasoning:

- Official site confirms Austin, TX, Zilker Park, October 2–4 and 9–11, 2026.
- Official site references 100+ performances across 9 stages over two weekends in Zilker Park.
- Official site offers hotel and festival packages, but this is attendee-facing.
- C3 Presents / Austin city-park two-weekend pattern makes it a useful annual anchor target, but the labor route and worker travel/lodging support remain unverified.

Next human action:

Verify C3/Live Nation labor route, Austin local/vendor pathway, staging/site ops vendors, and whether any paid travel or lodging exists for production labor.
