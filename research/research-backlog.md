# Research Expansion Backlog

Use this as the running queue for market research expansion.

## Priority 1 — Data extraction from current app

- Extract existing embedded `FESTIVALS` records from `index.html` into `data/festivals.json`.
- Extract existing embedded `COMPANIES` records from `index.html` into `data/companies.json`.
- Create source records for any existing URLs already in the app.
- Mark unsourced or older records as `needs-verification`.

## Priority 2 — High-value worker markets

Research and verify festival labor pathways for:

- California festival corridor.
- Las Vegas / Nevada event market.
- Florida electronic and rock festival market.
- Midwest summer festival market.
- Tennessee / Kentucky / Ohio country and rock corridor.
- Michigan festival and arena-adjacent work market.

## Priority 3 — Company/vendor expansion

Expand and verify:

- Labor staffing firms.
- IATSE locals by market.
- Staging and roof-system vendors.
- Lighting vendors.
- Audio vendors.
- Video/LED vendors.
- Barricade, fencing, flooring, tenting, power, and site operations vendors.

## Priority 4 — Work-finding intelligence

For each target market, collect:

- How workers actually apply.
- Whether the path is union, nonunion, vendor, payroll company, promoter, or staffing agency.
- Timing of application windows.
- Common credentials required.
- Travel/lodging notes.
- Publicly available rate or classification signals.

## Priority 5 — Production migration candidates

Promote records only after:

- At least one official or high-confidence source exists.
- Hiring pathway is clearly separated from event ownership.
- Research status is not vague.
- No old contract/vendor relationship is presented as current without verification.
