What Production Atlas is

Production Atlas is a public-facing work map for live-event production. It organizes festival dates, locations, production departments, public employer routes, and source references so contractors can decide which opportunities are worth researching.

The app is designed for workers who need practical public information: where events happen, when they happen, which departments are likely involved, and which official company or labor-route pages are relevant to the search.

Built for public research

Production Atlas uses public-facing event, company, source, and directory information. It does not publish non-public work details or personal contact information.

Built for planning

The map, calendar, schedule, and opportunity pages help workers compare timing, region, department fit, and public employer routes before deciding where to focus outreach.

Built for source review

Source links are centralized on the Sources page so opportunity and employer screens stay readable while public references remain available for review.
