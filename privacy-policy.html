Overview

Production Atlas is a static public website. It does not provide public visitor accounts, login forms, payment processing, private messaging, or a backend database for public visitors.

Information collected by this app

The public app is designed to display static research data. It does not intentionally collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, payment information, private employment information, or private contact records from visitors.

Local browser data

Some planning features may use browser-local storage to keep selections on the same device. Local browser data is not a public account and is not submitted to a Production Atlas backend.

Hosting and technical logs

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may process standard technical request information needed to deliver the website.

Contribute forms and third-party forms

When a visitor uses a linked or embedded contribution, feedback, or external form, that form may be processed by the third-party service that hosts it. Do not submit private contacts, pay information, hotel or lodging details, NDA material, client-sensitive information, private referrals, or non-public company information.

Third-party links

Production Atlas links to public event, employer, source, and organization websites. Those websites have their own privacy practices and terms. Review the linked site before submitting information there.

Contact and data questions

Use the Contact & Data Requests page for privacy, correction, or public information questions related to Production Atlas.
