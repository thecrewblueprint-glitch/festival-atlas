Cookies

Production Atlas does not intentionally set advertising cookies, tracking cookies, account cookies, payment cookies, or login cookies in the public static app.

Local storage

Some planning features may use local browser storage to remember selections on the same device, including the local Schedule page. This storage stays in the visitor’s browser and is not submitted to a Production Atlas backend.

Third-party websites and forms

External websites or embedded third-party forms linked from Production Atlas may use their own cookies or tracking technologies. Review each third-party site’s cookie and privacy notices before submitting information there.

Browser controls

Visitors can clear cookies or local storage through their browser settings. Clearing local storage may remove saved Schedule selections on that device.
